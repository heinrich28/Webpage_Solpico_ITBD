South Korea

South Korea is the global epicenter of K-Drama culture, captivating audiences with its compelling storytelling, breathtaking cinematography, and talented actors. The country's scenic locations have become iconic settings for romance, drama, and adventure.

Iconic K-Drama Filming Locations

Namsan Seoul Tower

A romantic hotspot featured in many K-Dramas, known for its love locks and breathtaking panoramic views of Seoul.

Han River

Famous for dreamy late-night walks and emotional scenes, the Han River is a must-visit for K-Drama lovers.

Bukchon Hanok Village

A traditional Korean village that has served as a backdrop for historical and modern dramas alike.

Petite France

A charming French-themed village featured in dramas like "My Love from the Star," adding a European touch to Korean storytelling.

How to Get There

Traveling to Seoul is convenient with multiple options available. You can fly into Incheon International Airport, which serves as the main gateway to the city. From the airport, take the Airport Railroad Express (AREX) for a fast and affordable trip to central Seoul. Alternatively, buses and taxis provide direct transport to various districts. If you're coming from nearby countries, high-speed trains and ferries offer additional travel options.
