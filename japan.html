Japan

Japan is a country that perfectly balances its vibrant city life with its world-renowned car culture. From the neon-lit streets of Tokyo to the underground car meets in Daikoku, the country offers a unique mix of modernity and automotive passion.

Iconic JDM Destinations

Tokyo

The bustling capital of Japan, Tokyo is known for its towering skyscrapers, cutting-edge technology, and electrifying nightlife. From shopping in Shibuya to exploring the historic streets of Asakusa, Tokyo has it all.

Yokohama

Located just south of Tokyo, Yokohama is a port city known for its beautiful waterfront, Chinatown, and of course, its deep roots in the JDM car scene.

Daikoku Parking Area

A legendary meeting spot for car enthusiasts, Daikoku is famous for its late-night car meets and showcasing some of the most unique and modified vehicles in Japan.

Suzuka Circuit

Home to numerous motorsport events, Suzuka Circuit is a must-visit for racing fans and a testament to Japan's rich automotive heritage.

How to Get There

Fly into Tokyo or Osaka international airports. Japan's extensive train system, including the Shinkansen (bullet train), makes it easy to explore the country. Renting a car is also an option for those looking to visit JDM hotspots.
